Society and bullying

The problem of bullying is a well-known social phenomenon and has been emphasized in modern society for quite some time. As we see from the research, bullying is effecting many people’s, from young children in schools to adults at workplaces. Unfortunately, the problem of bullying has sometimes tragic endings, if it’s not addressed properly.


There has been written a lot of literature on this subject both in Western world and worldwide. But still bullying is a part of our society lives and with introduction of new technology the problem of bullying seems to be growing and taking new forms.

On this webpage you will find information on how bullying still is a major problem in kindergartens, schools and work places. You will also find relevant solutions to the problems presented.

About Us

Together Against Bullying is a Norwegian organization, based in Oslo, that has a purpose of informing society on dangers of bullying. It was started in 2010 and has since then gained a lot of experience and recognition in this field.


On our website you’ll find information on bullying in kindergartens, schools and workplaces. We’re presenting the measures that can and should be taken in regards to bullying in all those places. In addition, as a solution to the problem of bullying we present our own free application for smartphones.


Our services include answering inquiries , arranging courses, information meetings in regards to bullying and harassment . Our services are going to be of use for younger audience, students, working people but also employees at kindergartens, schools and different workplaces where bullying or harassment is happening.
